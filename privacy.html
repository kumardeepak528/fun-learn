Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2, 2025

About Fun Learn: Picture & Sound

Fun Learn: Picture & Sound is a children's educational application designed to provide an interactive learning experience through images and corresponding sounds. This policy outlines how we (the app developer) handle information related to you and your children.

Information We Do Not Collect

We are committed to your privacy. Fun Learn: Picture & Sound does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data or identifiable information from any of its users—children or adults. Specifically:

  • No registration or login is required to access the app.
  • No name, email address, phone number, or physical address is collected.
  • No photographs, voice recordings, or any other personal content is collected or stored.
  • No device identifiers, IP addresses, or precise location data are collected.
  • No analytics tools or advertising IDs are integrated.
  • No information about app usage habits or user behavior is gathered.

Use of Third-Party Services

Fun Learn: Picture & Sound does not integrate with third-party services, frameworks, or SDKs that collect or process any data from users.

Children's Privacy & Parental Rights

This app is created for children and complies with international children's privacy laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. Since we do not collect any information, there is no exposure of children’s data to us or to any third parties.

If, in the future, personal information collection ever becomes necessary (for new features, accounts, etc.), we will update this policy and obtain verified parental consent before collecting such data.

Parents and guardians have the right at any time to review, request deletion of, or prevent further collection of their child's personal information. However, at present, since no such information is collected or stored, no action is necessary.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the app against unauthorized access or alterations. Since no personal data is collected, there is no user data at risk.

Legal Compliance

We comply with all applicable privacy laws and regulations. Should laws change or new data requirements arise, we will update this policy and, where required, notify users and obtain any necessary consents.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates. If we make significant changes, we will provide an in-app notification or other appropriate means.
